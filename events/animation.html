Animation

RULES

  • Individuals or a team of two can participate.
  • Competition will be conducted in two rounds, Story board layout and Animation using story board layout.
  • Each participant will receive a theme and must create a storyboard layout and then create an animation using the storyboard layout.
  • First round will have 1 1/2 hours and second round will have 3 hours.
  • There will be no shortlisting since the participants are limited to fifteen teams.
  • The animation should be 15 to 30 seconds in length. Framerate should be atleast 20 frames. Usage of any templates are not allowed.
  • Participants can bring their laptop or system will be provided.
  • Participants should not use AI based softwares and everything should be made by the participants itself.
  • There will be no shortlisting since the participants are limited to fifteen teams.
  • Evaluation will be conducted on the final submission only. Venue: Animation Lab
Registration fee: 200 [Refreshments will be provided]
